# Day 1 Exercises

## Interview Questions

### What does SOC stand for?

Security Operations Center.

### What are the three components of the CIA Triad?

Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability.

### What is the main responsibility of a SOC Analyst?

To monitor systems, detect threats, investigate alerts, and respond to security incidents.

### Difference between Blue Team and Red Team?

Blue Team defends systems, while Red Team performs simulated attacks to test security.

### Why are system logs important?

System logs record activities and help analysts detect suspicious behavior, investigate incidents, and understand what happened during an attack.

## Optional Research

### Firewall

A firewall filters incoming and outgoing network traffic based on security rules.

### Antivirus

Antivirus software detects, blocks, and removes malicious software from a computer.

### IDS (Intrusion Detection System)

An IDS monitors network or system activity and alerts administrators when suspicious behavior is detected.

### IPS (Intrusion Prevention System)

An IPS not only detects malicious activity but also automatically blocks or prevents attacks in real time.
